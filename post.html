When Saturn is connected to marriage, people immediately panic. They assume denial, loneliness, or lifelong delay. That assumption is incorrect.

Saturn does not deny marriage. Saturn delays marriage until emotional maturity, self-responsibility, and inner stability are achieved.

In charts where Saturn influences the 7th house, Venus, or marriage significators, relationships begin early but do not sustain unless they are emotionally grounded.
